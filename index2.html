App 1 - Sentence Sorting Activity

This is an interactive smartboard activity for Day 5 of Predictable Chart Writing: Be The Sentence. Enter one of the student’s sentence in the box and click Create Sorting Activity. Ask students to arrange the words to match their sentence or indicate to a partner how they want the words ordered. Repeat with each of the students’ sentences. . Click the link above to access it.
